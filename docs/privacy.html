Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Dysmorphia (“we,” “our,” or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Dysmorphia mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

a. Account Information

We collect your email address to create and manage your account. We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, or contact list.

b. User-Generated Content

You may voluntarily provide:

  • Progress photos
  • Notes and journal entries
  • Body measurements and fitness-related metrics

This content is private by default and visible only to your account.

c. App Preferences

We store preferences such as your selected check-in frequency and reminder schedule (day and time).

d. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited technical data such as app version, device type, and crash logs. This data is used only to improve app stability and performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core app functionality
  • Store and display your progress data
  • Securely sync your data across devices
  • Improve app reliability and user experience

We do not sell or share your personal data for advertising purposes.

4. Photos & Health-Related Data

Dysmorphia allows users to upload personal photos and body-related data for self-tracking purposes. All photos and metrics are:

  • Stored securely
  • Private and visible only to your account
  • Not shared with other users

You may delete your photos and entries at any time within the app.

5. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the app, including:

  • Supabase – authentication, database, and secure storage
  • Expo – app framework and infrastructure

These services process data only as required to provide app functionality.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You may:

  • Delete individual entries and photos within the app
  • Delete your account at any time from the app’s Settings page

When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Dysmorphia is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date above.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:
support@dysmorphia.app
